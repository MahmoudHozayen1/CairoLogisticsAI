Learning-to-Route

Back to AI

A pointer-network-style policy picks the next stop by paying attention to every remaining parcel, scoring each with learned weights and turning the scores into probabilities (a softmax). It was trained end-to-end with REINFORCE and a greedy-rollout baseline — the same recipe behind neural combinatorial optimisers — implemented in pure NumPy so it deploys without a deep-learning framework.
