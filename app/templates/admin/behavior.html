Each courier's shift is reconstructed from a simulated GPS trace. A stop-detection pass turns the raw pings into driving, delivery, idle and break segments, which become behavioural features. K-Means then groups shifts into interpretable personas, and every score is explained with the features that drove it (standardised z-scores). Pure NumPy + scikit-learn, so it deploys without extra services.
